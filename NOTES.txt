Pin requirements

- 16MHz crystal                                     PB6, PB7
- RX/TX for USB-to-serial converter circuit (more?) PD0, PD1
- 3 PWM out for bar graph displays                  PD3, PD5, PD6
- 1 digital out for low-power flicker				PD7
- 4 digital out for shift registers                 PB5 (CLK), PB3 (MOSI), PB0 (CLR), PB1 (REG)
- 1 analogue in for DSKY keyboard                   PC0
